Matthew J Barnhart, mastering engineer in Chicago, Illinois.

Hello.

My name is Matthew and I'm a mastering engineer. I love helping people make the records of their dreams.

I'm a staff engineer at Bob Weston's Chicago Mastering Service where since 2012 I've mastered and cut vinyl master lacquers for hundreds of releases from Merge Records, Sub Pop, Matador, Fat Possum, Polyvinyl, Jagjaguwar, Numero Group, Goner, and many, many others.

I've also recorded and mixed a whole lot of records (mostly at The Echo Lab and Electrical Audio).

Other things: I co-founded The Echo Lab recording studio in Denton, Texas. I'm a member of the bands Mint Mile and Tre Orsi. I've been Superchunk's tour manager and live sound engineer since 2010, and have done the same with Destroyer, Bob Mould, The New Pornographers, the Mountain Goats, Ex Hex, Jawbox, CHVRCHES, and others. I handle the location recording for live episodes of the Judge John Hodgman Podcast.

I'd love to help you on your next project. Get in touch.
